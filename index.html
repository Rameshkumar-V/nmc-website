Who We Are

About NMC

Our College

Nehru Memorial College is an Autonomous, Aided institution affiliated to Bharathidhasan University, established in 1967. The college offers various UG & PG Programmes and promotes research & innovation in teaching-learning processes across various disciplines, with a special focus on Computer Science and Instrumentation.

Vision

"For Knowledge, Justice and Peace."

Mission

  • → Impart the right kind of knowledge among rural students with the aid of computers and other equipment.
  • → Ensure social justice through academic and non-academic activities — cultural, NSS, NCC, YRC, Gender Club etc.
  • → Stabilize inner peace through meditation and Yoga practice to ensure peace among rural people.

Principal's Message

Principal
Nehru Memorial College
Read Full Message →

Nehru Memorial College has been a beacon of quality education for over five decades. As an institution that pioneered Computer Science education in India, we have always been at the forefront of academic excellence and innovation.

Our NAAC A+ accreditation and NIRF ranking of 101–150 are testaments to our commitment to providing world-class education to rural students. We continue to strive for excellence in research, academics, and holistic development.
